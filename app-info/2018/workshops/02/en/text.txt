/** TITLE **/
Hollie Hood-Mincey

/** SUBTITLE **/
Contemporary Gospel from Baltimore

/** INFO **/
Hollie Hood-Mincey is the founder and director of Singing Sensations Youth Choir. She is a vocal music teacher at the Baltimore City School System, and has worked more than twenty-five years in the field of education, eight of them as director. She provides a great quantity and quality of knowledge and experiences in the field of education and music.
