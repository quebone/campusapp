/** SUBTITLE **/
The roots of Gospel from Califòrnia

/** INFO **/
Linda Tillery and The Cultural Heritage Choir is a vocal ensemble nominated at the Grammy©, whose mission is to help preserve and share the musical traditions of the African diaspora, with special emphasis on music from the South of the United States. Connecting this music with its origins in West Africa and the Caribbean.
Terrance Kelly has been called from "a man of great passion and conviction" to a "great master" to "an absolute musical genius." And to demonstrate this, he has developed extensive credentials both in Gospel music as well as in jazz. His operatic rank and emotion make him a popular soloist and a consummate director, composer and arranger.
